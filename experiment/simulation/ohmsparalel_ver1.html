INSTRUCTION

  1. Connect the components as mentioned below:
    L1-L3, L3-L10, L4-L11, L8-L11, L10-L7, L6-L9, L9-L12, L4-L5.(For eg. click on 1 and then drag to 3 and so on.)
  2. Click on 'Check Connection' button to check the connections.
  3. If connected wrong, double click on the wrong connection. Else click on 'Delete all connection' button to erase all the connections.
  4. Set DC voltage(0-30 V).
  5. Here Resistances are kept parallelly. Set the resistance R1(1- 20 kohm) value and set resistance R2(1 -20 kohm).
  6. Voltmeter is placed parallel to resistor and ammeter series with resistor.
  7. Now note the Voltmeter and Ammeter reading for DC voltageand click on 'Add to table' button.
  8. Increase the DC voltage by 2 factor and note Voltmeter and Ammeter Readings. Keeping resistance value constant
  9. Click on 'Plot' button to plot the V-I graph to verify Ohm's Law
  10. Repeat step 2 to 6 for another set of resistance value.
